    Opening Hours is the context aware business hours plugin for WordPress. I started this project in 2013 mainly for fun and to get to know the way the WordPress plugin and theme repositories work.

    Over time it - by accident - became the most used business hours plugin on the WordPress.org platform with over 10.000 active installations and 60.000 downloads.

    It stands out by time context aware widgets and shortcodes, multi-venue capability and extensive developer APIs for individual adjustments or third party extensions.
